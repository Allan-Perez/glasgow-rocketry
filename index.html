Scotland's leading student society for the development of the space industry

What do we do?

We design, test, and build in-house small-scale rockets that we launch to suborbital heights, all led and done by Glasgow University students of engineering, physics, computer science, and fields.

Why do we do it?

The space industry standard of the skills of the workforce is always evolving, and while in some places it's tending to zero, we decided to do something about it, and give our future workforce an opportunity to stand out and have hands-on experience on high tech research, development, and manufacturing skills. We found this to be the way to encourage the students to thrive for the stars and not settle with just theoretical work.

What are our values?

In order to accomplish our goal, we guide our decision making by the same compass we guide our careers: Work harder than what you think you can, learn more than what you think you can, and love it while doing it, because if you aim to the stars you will at least land among them.
